Background

I started coding at 13 and built my first medical device at 17. That maker instinct never left — it just scaled up. At Karolinska I researched bioentrepreneurship alongside Niklas Zennström and Andreas Ehn. With INTOINO I raised €0.5M, won 15+ international awards, and was named Top 3 startup worldwide by The Economist.

At Technogym I led the renewal of an entire multinational's digital offering as Agile Tribe Lead — delivering Apple GymKit, Samsung Galaxy Watch and IBM Watson integrations, then led a 15+ AI team. At BKN301 I now lead a €30M digital banking venture in MENA, managing 4 Senior PMs and building a full B2C product stack from scratch.

When I'm not building products, I restore vintage vans from scratch — same process, different materials. It keeps the builder honest.
